About Me

I am a seasoned multimedia production specialist and videographer with over a decade of experience in broadcast, digital, and OTT media. I specialize in post-production workflows, audiovisual technology, and end-to-end video production. I am highly proficient in Adobe Creative Suite, Avid, DaVinci Resolve, and Dalet systems, and I have produced content for global audiences across television, radio, and digital platforms. At Voice of America, I have led projects that support live news coverage, interviews with world leaders, and multilingual content distribution. I am skilled in operating ENG cameras, directing live studio productions, and creating visually compelling content for both traditional broadcasts and social media. Fluent in English and Spanish, I also excel at technical troubleshooting, equipment maintenance, and training colleagues, consistently contributing to improved efficiency and high editorial standards.
